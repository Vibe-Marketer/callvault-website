Both platforms achieve **85-90% transcription accuracy** in ideal conditions. Performance drops with accents, overlapping dialogue, or background noise. [Source: Fireflies Review 2025]

**Best practices checklist:**

- ✓ Minimize background noise and interruptions  
- ✓ Speak clearly and avoid rapid overlapping dialogue
- ✓ Test accuracy with your specific accent/speaking style

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which tool has better transcription accuracy for coaching calls?

Both achieve 85-90% accuracy in controlled conditions with clear audio and native speakers. Performance drops significantly with strong accents, overlapping dialogue, or background noise common in coaching environments.


### How much do CallVault and Fireflies.ai cost for small coaching practices?

Fireflies.ai Pro costs $10/seat annually with unlimited transcription minutes. CallVault pricing varies based on coaching-specific features, making Fireflies more predictable for budget planning.


### Can these tools handle group coaching sessions?

Fireflies.ai supports up to 3 concurrent calls, ideal for group sessions or back-to-back coaching. CallVault focuses on individual coaching workflows with specialized client management features.


### Are these tools compliant for sensitive coaching recordings?

Both offer GDPR compliance and encryption for call recordings. However, coaches handling healthcare, legal, or financial coaching should verify specific compliance requirements with each provider.
